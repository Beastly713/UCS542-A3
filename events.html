Explore Competitions

Choose from coding, robotics, design, security, and trivia challenges. Read the summaries below, then secure your spot.

Coding & Engineering

  1. 24-Hour Hackathon

    Build a functional prototype that solves a campus or community problem.

    • Team size: 2–5
    • When: Sept 12 10:00 — Sept 13 10:00
    • Venue: Innovation Lab, Block B
    • Judging: Impact, usability, technical depth, presentation

  2. Line-Follower Robotics

    Design and tune a robot to navigate a dynamic track with speed and precision.

    • Team size: 1–3
    • When: Sept 13 14:00–17:00
    • Venue: Mechanical Workshop Arena
    • Rules: Standard 12V limit; no external assistance; two trial runs

  3. UI/UX Design Sprint

    Create a mobile app concept from brief to clickable mockups—storyboard, wireframe, and present.

    • Team size: 1–3
    • When: Sept 12 13:00–17:00
    • Venue: Design Studio, Block C
    • Deliverables: Wireframes, style tile, 5-minute pitch

Programming Challenges

  • Code Golf

    Solve problems in the fewest characters possible. Shorter is better.

    • Team size: Solo
    • When: Sept 14 10:00–12:00
    • Venue: CS Lab 204
    • Languages: Any compiled or interpreted language

  • Capture The Flag (CTF)

    Compete in a jeopardy-style cybersecurity CTF covering web, crypto, forensics, and reverse engineering.

    • Team size: 1–4
    • When: Sept 13 18:00–22:00
    • Venue: Network Lab
    • Rules: No attacking infrastructure; flags are case-sensitive

Quizzes & Social

  • Tech Quiz Bowl

    Fast-paced trivia on computing history, AI breakthroughs, and general tech.

    • Team size: 2–3
    • When: Sept 14 14:00–16:00
    • Venue: Auditorium A
    • Format: Prelims (MCQs) → Finals (buzzer)
