Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma students from any accredited institution are welcome. Some events may specify team-size or skill prerequisites—see the Events page.

How do I register for multiple events?

Use the multi-select on the registration form. On desktop, hold Ctrl/Cmd to select more than one; on mobile, tap each event.

Is there a registration fee?

No fee for most competitions. If an event has a nominal fee or requires a deposit for kits, it will be indicated in its description.

What should I bring?

Valid student ID, your laptop and charger, and any hardware required for robotics events. Power strips are recommended for teams.

Can I modify my team after registration?

Yes, up to 48 hours before the event start time. Contact the organizing team at techfest@college.example.

Will participation certificates be provided?

Yes, e-certificates will be emailed to all participants after the festival concludes.
